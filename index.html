Education for Meaningful Lives.

Glenaeon is a co-educational Preschool to Year 12 school across three leafy campuses, where children grow through a meaningful, creative and nature-connected education that nurtures purpose and possibility.

Innovation in Every Discovery.

Our students engage in cutting-edge research and hands-on experiments across diverse disciplines, fostering critical thinking skills that prepare them to tackle tomorrow's challenges with confidence and creativity.

Creativity in Action and Growth.

Students explore artistic expression through multimedia projects and performance arts, developing both technical skills and creative confidence while building lasting connections with peers and mentors.

Meaningful Lives.

We acknowledge and pay respect to the traditional owners of the land, the Cammeraygal people of the Gu-Ring-Gai Nation. Our school stands on their ancestral lands. We pay respect to the wisdom and traditions of the aboriginal custodians of country whose culture and customs have nurtured this Land since The Dreaming. We honour these past practices of the Cammeraygal people and pay our respects to the elders past, present and emerging, and the ancient spirituality of their Ancestors.
