A life of character AND CONTRIBUTION

The premise of Steiner education is that teachers help children and adolescents become independent thinkers and creative individuals.

For over 65 years, Glenaeon Rudolf Steiner School has helped children and young adults find meaning and direction in their lives. Through a considered balance of academic, aesthetic, artisan, altruistic and active wilderness, we equip students with everything they need to lead a life of character and contribution.

The Five A's of a

Glenaeon Education

A Glenaeon education is based on five foundational programs that interweave through the curriculum from Kindergarten to Year 12.

Academic

Glenaeon provides a rigorous academic program that challenges students to think critically and develop a deep understanding of core subjects.

Our curriculum integrates traditional academic excellence with innovative teaching methods that inspire curiosity and lifelong learning.

Students are supported to achieve their personal best through individualized attention and a comprehensive assessment framework.

Explore Academic Programs

Artisan

Hands-on learning through practical arts and crafts develops fine motor skills, patience, and an appreciation for quality workmanship.

Students engage in woodwork, metalwork, textiles, and other craft activities that connect them to traditional skills and creative expression.

These practical experiences build confidence and provide a tangible sense of accomplishment through creating beautiful, functional objects.

Explore Artisan Programs

Aesthetic

The aesthetic program nurtures students' appreciation for beauty and artistic expression through music, drama, visual arts, and movement.

Regular performances, exhibitions, and creative projects allow students to develop their artistic voice and share their work with the community.

This holistic approach to arts education enriches the learning experience and fosters emotional intelligence and cultural awareness.

Explore Aesthetic Programs

Active Wilderness

Glenaeon builds resilience through healthy outdoor activities in an age-appropriate sequence.

A sophisticated outdoor education program taking students on experiences from a local setting to adventures across Australia, and offering the Duke of Edinburgh Award.

A range of curricular and co-curricular sporting activities ensures all students are supported in their physical and emotional development.

Explore Outdoor Education

Altruistic

Service learning and community engagement are integral to a Glenaeon education, fostering empathy, social responsibility, and leadership.

Students participate in meaningful service projects that connect them with local and global communities, developing a sense of purpose and contribution.

Through these experiences, students learn the value of giving back and develop skills in collaboration, problem-solving, and compassion.

Meaningful Lives.

We acknowledge and pay respect to the traditional owners of the land, the Cammeraygal people of the Gu-Ring-Gai Nation. Our school stands on their ancestral lands. We pay respect to the wisdom and traditions of the aboriginal custodians of country whose culture and customs have nurtured this Land since The Dreaming. We honour these past practices of the Cammeraygal people and pay our respects to the elders past, present and emerging, and the ancient spirituality of their Ancestors.
